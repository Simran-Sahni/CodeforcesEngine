Today, Eric teaches independent set and edge-induced subgraph.Given a graph $$$G=(V,E)$$$, an independent set is a subset of vertices $$$V' \subset V$$$ such that for every pair $$$u,v \in V'$$$, $$$(u,v) \not \in E$$$ (i.e. no edge in $$$E$$$ connects two vertices from $$$V'$$$).An edge-induced subgraph consists of a subset of edges $$$E' \subset E$$$ and all the vertices in the original graph that are incident on at least one edge in the subgraph.Given $$$E' \subset E$$$, denote $$$G[E']$$$ the edge-induced subgraph such that $$$E'$$$ is the edge set of the subgraph.
